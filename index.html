What is Web Hosting?

Web hosting is a service that allows individuals or businesses to publish their website on the internet. A hosting provider stores your website’s files and data on a server that is accessible to users via the internet.

How do I choose the right hosting plan for my website?

The right hosting plan depends on factors such as your website’s size, traffic, and technical requirements. For smaller websites, shared hosting is ideal, while VPS or dedicated hosting is more suitable for high-traffic or resource-intensive websites.

What is the difference between shared hosting and VPS hosting?

In shared hosting, multiple websites share the same server resources, making it cost-effective but less flexible. VPS hosting, on the other hand, provides more resources and control, as your website gets a virtual private server with dedicated resources.
